Research

My work integrates UX research, AI literacy, and community-engaged pedagogy. I study the digital rhetoric of emerging technologies, focusing on algorithmic rhetoric, posthumanism, social justice, and design ethics. Using qualitative and quantitative methods, I examine how AI and UX shape communication across academic, professional, and community settings.
