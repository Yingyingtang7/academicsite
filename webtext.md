ENGL 3331 AI Writing for Communities  
Online: Spring 2026 (1 section)  
An undergraduate course examining how generative AI tools support professional, technical, and community-based writing. Topics include AI literacy, ethical and rhetorical decision-making, and human–AI collaboration. Projects include prompt-engineering logs, AI-assisted professional documents, data-driven reports, and community-oriented writing.  
TCOM 5385 User Experience Research and Design  
Online: Fall 2025 (1 section)  
A graduate-level course introducing UX theories, usability research, and user-centered design. Projects include heuristic evaluations, usability test plans, team-based research reports, and professional presentations.  
ENGL 3330 Introduction to Technical Writing  
In-person: Fall 2024 (2 sections), Fall 2025 (1 section)  
An undergraduate course on writing across technical genres, including job-application materials, correspondence, usability reports, proposals, and technical-writing style, with attention to audience analysis and document design.  
ENGL 4340 Professional Style and Editing  
Online: Fall 2024 (1 section)  
An advanced undergraduate course on style and editing in technical and professional communication, including copyediting, style analyses, and client-based editing projects, with attention to ethics in editing.  
TCOM 5380 Document Design  
Online: Fall 2024 (1 section)  
A graduate-level course on typography, layout, visual rhetoric, and accessibility, emphasizing user-centered design and social justice in document design.
