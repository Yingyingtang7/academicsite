    ENGL 3331 AI Writing for Communities

    Online: Spring 2026 (1 section)

    I designed and taught this undergraduate course to help students explore how generative AI tools can enhance professional, technical, and community-based writing. The course covers AI literacy, ethical and rhetorical decision-making, and effective human–AI collaboration.
